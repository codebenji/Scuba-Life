Scuba Diving in the Europe

Mediterranean: The Mediterranean Sea offers diverse diving opportunities, with destinations like the Greek Islands, the Costa Brava in Spain, and the French Riviera providing easy access to colorful marine life and historical wrecks.

Red Sea (Egypt): The Red Sea is known for its warm waters, vibrant coral reefs, and an abundance of marine life. Places like Sharm El Sheikh and Dahab are popular dive destinations..

Croatia: The Adriatic Sea along the Croatian coast features clear waters and interesting underwater formations. You can explore wrecks, caves, and coral reefs.

Malta: The Maltese archipelago is known for its historical wrecks and underwater caves. It's a great spot for wreck diving enthusiasts.

Azores (Portugal): The Azores offer unique diving experiences in the middle of the Atlantic Ocean, with opportunities to encounter pelagic species, underwater volcanic formations, and deep-sea diving.
